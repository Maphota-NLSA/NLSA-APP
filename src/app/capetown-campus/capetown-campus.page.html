Getting to the library

By Bus The library is well located within Cape Town, a short distance from many city attractions. For those wishing to take the bus to the National Library of South Africa, the MyCiti bus offers a convenient and affordable option.
Bus Timetable

By Car For those arriving by car, the National Library is accessible via the following GPS coordinates: 33°55’30.1"S 18°25’06.4"E

From the N1 Highway
  • Take Exit 12 towards Cape Town Centre/Sea Point.
  • Turn left onto National Highway 1.
  • Turn right onto Wale Street.
  • The library is located on the right-hand side of the street
 From the M3 Highway
  • Take Exit 10 towards Cape Town Centre.
  • Turn left onto Somerset Road.
  • Turn right onto Darling Street.
  • Turn left onto Queen Victoria Street.
  • Turn right onto Wale Street.
  • The library is located on the right-hand side of the street.

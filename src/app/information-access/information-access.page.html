Information Access Services (IAS) provides access to the NLSA’s collections and resources. IAS does this by
Providing reference and information services to the public.

  • access to the NLSA’s digital collections.
  • Facilitating interlibrary loans.
  • Providing training and education on information literacy.

IAS plays a vital role in ensuring that the NLSA’s collections are accessible to everyone. Its work is essential for research, education, and developing a knowledge-based society.
